# Privacy Policy for Apsidyne

**Effective Date:** 2025-12-28

## 1. Introduction
"Apsidyne" (hereinafter referred to as "this Extension") is a Chrome extension designed to help users manage text snippets. This Privacy Policy describes how your data is handled.

## 2. Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers.**
All data (snippets you create) is stored locally on your device using the Chrome Storage API.

## 3. Data Usage
The data stored by this Extension is used solely for the purpose of providing the functionality of the Extension (displaying and copying snippets).

## 4. Third-Party Services
This Extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.
